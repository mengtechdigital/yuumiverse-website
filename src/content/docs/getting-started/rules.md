## 1. Don't Be a Jerk

This is the golden rule. Harassment, hate speech, slurs, and targeted toxicity will get you banned. We're here to have fun.

## 2. No Cheating

- No hacked clients
- No x-ray (including texture packs)
- No auto-clickers or macros for PvP/fishing
- No exploiting bugs — report them instead

Our [Anglers](/plugins/anglers/) plugin has built-in anti-auto-fishing. Don't try to bypass it.

## 3. Griefing & Raiding

- **Griefing random builds is not allowed**
- **Raiding is allowed** during declared wars between Realms (see [Realms plugin](/plugins/realms/))
- Don't destroy things just to be destructive

## 4. Realm Politics

- Alliances, wars, and betrayals are part of the game
- Keep it in-character — don't let RP drama become real drama
- No spawn-camping or repeated killing without RP context

## 5. Chat

- English in global chat so everyone can participate
- Keep spam and excessive caps to a minimum
- Marriage drama broadcasts are funny — actual drama is not

## Enforcement

Mods have final say. Bans can be appealed via Discord. We use [CoreProtect](https://www.spigotmc.org/resources/coreprotect.8631/) to log everything, so don't think you can get away with sneaky griefing.
