Welcome to **Yuumiverse** — a semi-vanilla Minecraft SMP where plugins fundamentally change how you play Minecraft.

## What is Yuumiverse?

Yuumiverse is a **community-first** Minecraft server. We have:

- **0 mods** — everything runs on custom plugins
- **No pay2win** — no ranked kits, no crate keys, no advantage for sale
- **9 custom plugins** that add entirely new mechanics to vanilla survival
- **A living world** with politics, territory, and drama

## Quick Start

1. **Join the server** — `play.yuumiverse.gg` on Java 1.21
2. **Read the [Rules](/getting-started/rules/)**
3. **Join our [Discord](https://discord.gg/yuumiverse)** — this is where the community lives
4. **Pick a plugin to master** — check out the [Plugins](/plugins/yuumi/) section

## Server Philosophy

> *"Vanilla Minecraft is the canvas. Our plugins are the paint. You create the story."*

We believe the best Minecraft experiences come from player-driven stories, not pre-written quests. Our plugins give you new tools and systems to play with, but what you do with them is entirely up to you.

Whether you want to:
- Build a massive empire with [Realms](/plugins/realms/)
- Become the server's best fisher with [Anglers](/plugins/anglers/)
- Terrafrom mountains with [Terraform](/plugins/terraform/)
- Or just vibe as someone's companion with [Yuumi](/plugins/yuumi/)

There's a place for you here.
