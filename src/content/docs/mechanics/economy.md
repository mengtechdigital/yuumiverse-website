---
title: Economy
description: How money works in Yuumiverse.
---

Yuumiverse runs a **player-driven economy**. There are no admin shops or infinite money fountains.

## Getting Money

- **Selling items to other players** — Use chest shops or trade directly
- **Fishing** — The [Anglers](/plugins/anglers/) plugin gives valuable treasure
- **Farming** — [CropFarm](/plugins/cropfarm/) crops sell for premium prices
- **Mining** — Sell rare ores and gems to other players
- **Services** — Charge for building, terraforming, or protection

## Spending Money

- **Player shops** — Buy from other players' chest shops
- **Realm taxes** — Some realms charge for living in their territory
- **Terraforming services** — Pay someone with tools to reshape your land

## No Admin Shops

We don't have `/sell` or admin shops that generate infinite money. The economy is entirely player-to-player. This means:
- Prices are set by supply and demand
- Scarcity actually matters
- Player specialization is encouraged

## Tips

- Early-game fishing is one of the fastest ways to build capital
- Find a niche — if nobody is selling enchanted books, become the book guy
- Realm economies can become powerful — a well-run realm with shops attracts citizens
