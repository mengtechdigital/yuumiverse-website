Nightfall makes the night something to actually fear. The surface becomes deadly after dark.

## Day / Night Cycle

- **Days are longer** — plenty of time to build, explore, and gather
- **Nights are shorter but lethal** — you do NOT want to be caught outside unprepared

## No Sleep-Skipping

Beds are disabled for skipping night. You can set your spawn point, but the night will pass naturally. This means:
- Mob grinders work better
- Nighttime resource gathering is risky but rewarding
- Everyone experiences the danger together

## Buffed Night Mobs

At night, hostile mobs gain significant buffs:
- **Runner Zombies** — Zombies move faster and can catch you if you're not sprinting
- **Increased damage** — All hostile mobs deal more damage
- **Increased spawn rates** — More mobs spawn on the surface
- **Extended follow range** — Mobs will chase you from much further away

## Commands

| Command | Permission | Description |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| `/nightfall status` | `nightfall.admin` | Check day/night status and mob modifiers |
| `/nightfall day` | `nightfall.admin` | Force day (admin) |
| `/nightfall night` | `nightfall.admin` | Force night (admin) |
| `/nightfall mobs` | `nightfall.admin` | Toggle buffed mobs on/off |
| `/nightfall reload` | `nightfall.admin` | Reload config |

## Tips

- Build walls and lighting around your base early
- The night is short — if you can survive the first wave, dawn comes fast
- Runner zombies are the biggest threat. Always keep food for sprinting
- Underground is relatively safe, but caves still spawn mobs normally
