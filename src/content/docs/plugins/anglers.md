Anglers overhauls fishing to reward active players and punish AFK fish farms.

## Anti-Auto-Fishing

The plugin detects common auto-fishing patterns:
- Perfect re-cast timing
- Lack of camera movement between casts
- Excessive catch rates

Caught auto-fishing? Your catch is cancelled and you may be flagged for mods.

## Buffed Drops

Active fishers get significantly better loot:
- Higher chance of treasure (enchanted books, name tags, saddles)
- Rare custom fish with lore
- Bonus XP for "perfect" catches (reeling in at the right moment)

## Commands

| Command | Permission | Description |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| `/anglers guide` | `anglers.guide` | Open the fishing guide GUI |
| `/anglers reload` | `anglers.admin` | Reload config |

## Tips

- Move your camera between casts — the plugin tracks engagement
- The longer you actively fish in one spot, the better your luck modifier gets
- Use `/anglers guide` to see the full loot table and your personal stats
