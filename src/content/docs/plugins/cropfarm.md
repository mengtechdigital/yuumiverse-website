CropFarm adds a full custom agriculture system on top of vanilla farming. Plant seeds that grow into ores, mob drops, building blocks, dyes, wool, and even endgame treasure.

## How It Works

1. **Craft seeds** using the item you want to grow (e.g. 1 Diamond → 4 Diamond Seeds)
2. **Right-click farmland** while holding seeds to plant
3. Crops grow on a **timer** — light and water don't matter
4. **Bone meal is blocked** for balance
5. Break the crop when fully grown to receive the output plus one returned seed
6. Breaking early returns the seed — nothing is lost
7. Harvested items and XP go **straight to your inventory**

:::tip
You must use a **hoe** to harvest tracked crops. Breaking with anything else is cancelled.
:::

## Tiers

Every crop belongs to a tier that defines its grow time, planting cap, and XP drops.

| Tier | Grow Time | Per-Player Cap | XP/ Harvest | Color |
|------|-----------|----------------|-------------|-------|

## Crafting Recipes

Every crop uses the same recipe pattern: **1 input item → seeds**.

| Input | Seeds Produced |
|-------|---------------|
| Most items | 4 seeds |
| Rare / Epic items | 1–2 seeds |
| Common bulk items | 4–8 seeds |

Example: Place 1 Diamond in a crafting grid → receive 4 Diamond Seeds.

## Seed Bags

Seeds are stored and traded in **Seed Bags** — portable containers that hold up to a stack of one seed type. Use `/cropfarm bag` to manage your collection. Admins can spawn tiered bags with `/cropfarm bag give`.

## Commands

| Command | Permission | Description |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| `/cropfarm menu` | `cropfarm.menu` | Open the CropFarm menu |
| `/cropfarm bag` | `cropfarm.menu` | Open your seed bag inventory |
| `/cropfarm give <crop> [amount] [player]` | `cropfarm.give` | Admin: give seeds |
| `/cropfarm reload` | `cropfarm.reload` | Reload config |

## Tips

- Floating nametags above crops show progress (`(3/7)`) — no need to guess
- Crops are protected from water, pistons, explosions, and mob trampling
- Direct-to-inventory harvesting means zero entity lag on large farms
- Endgame seeds are disabled by default — ask admins if they're enabled on this server
